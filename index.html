About

Sound as a space
we can enter.

Carolyne (Chuowen) Liang is a Boston-based composer and sound designer working across electronic music, spatial audio, installation, and moving image.

Currently studying Electronic Production & Design at Berklee College of Music, her practice brings together field recording, multichannel systems, synthesis, and visual media to explore how sound reshapes attention and place.
